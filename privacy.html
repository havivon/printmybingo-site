Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Print My Bingo (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our bingo card generator.

To improve usability, the site may store settings (board size, theme options, word list) in your browser’s local storage. This remains on your device unless you clear your browser data.

We may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic and improve performance. Analytics typically reports aggregated metrics (page views, browser type) rather than directly identifying you.

We may show ads using Google AdSense or similar services. Advertising partners may use cookies to personalize ads and measure performance. You can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.
