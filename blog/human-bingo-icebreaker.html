Human Bingo Icebreaker: Best Prompts for Teams

Human Bingo turns introductions into movement and conversation. It’s a reliable icebreaker for workshops, offsites, onboarding, and community meetups.

How Human Bingo works

Each square contains a statement. Players must find someone who matches it and write their name in the square (or mark it). The first to complete a line wins.

Inclusive prompt ideas

  • Speaks 2+ languages
  • Has visited 5+ countries
  • Plays a musical instrument
  • Has a pet
  • Enjoys hiking
  • Prefers tea over coffee

What to avoid

Avoid sensitive topics (health, politics, religion, income). Keep prompts fun and broad so everyone can participate comfortably.

Best layout

Use 4×4 for a fast round (10–15 minutes). For larger groups, 5×5 adds variety.
