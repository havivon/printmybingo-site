Birthday Party Bingo (Kids): Themes, Word Lists & Tips

Birthday bingo is a perfect 10–15 minute activity that keeps a group entertained while you set up food, gifts, or the next game. Use Word mode to build themed boards and keep printing simple.

Pick a board size by age

For ages 4–6: 3×3 or 4×4. For ages 7+: 4×4 or 5×5. Add a FREE center for younger kids to increase wins and smiles.

Easy party themes (copy-paste)

Animals: dog, cat, lion, elephant, giraffe, monkey, zebra, tiger, bear, dolphin.

Superhero: cape, mask, hero, shield, power, mission, team, villain, lightning, gadget.

Sports: ball, goal, team, coach, win, score, practice, stadium, jersey, whistle.

Make it fast and fair

  • Use line wins only (row/column/diagonal) for quick rounds.
  • Plan 2–3 rounds instead of one long game.
  • Have small prizes ready (stickers, candy, mini toys).

Add your party branding

A small logo or light background can make printouts feel custom. Keep contrast high so words remain readable.

FAQ

Do I need an account? No—Print My Bingo works without signup.

Can I use words instead of numbers? Yes—use Word mode and paste a list of terms.

How do I export? Use your browser’s Print dialog to save as PDF or print directly.
